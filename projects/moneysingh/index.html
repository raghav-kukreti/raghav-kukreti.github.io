BIOGRAPHY

Yo Yo Money Singh is younger brother of popular Bollywood singer and rapper Yo Yo Honey Singh. Recorded in English, Money’s songs revolve around a deep house, trance and hip hop baseline with kicks from Punjabi songs and Bhangra music. He is currently based in India.


Born in the time when techno pop was gaining traction, Money Singh, whose real name is Videsh Singh, got quickly into listening to vintage greats like Depeche Mode and Kraftwerk. He rapidly picked up rhythms and started reproducing them on his synthesizer. His genius was quickly recognized by the people living around him and got him to sign a debut album with Runnin’ Records at the tender age of 15. From then, he has released various new songs which have been released under the same label with much success on SoundCloud and Beatport.


He has incorporated guitars and other more traditional pop influences that particularly appeal to a teenage audience. With distinct influence of Kraftwerk, Yo Yo Money Singh’s music is just classy and clean and is direct continuation of the European synthesizer tradition. Pursuing a more dance-orientated sound, his album Party, Sharty and Money reached the n°1 in the album charts in India. His newest album, Money All Night or simply M.A.N is a collaboration with DJ, Armin Vaan Buuren and makes it a head banging experience for all listeners.
