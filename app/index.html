ABOUT
Yo Yo Money Singh is the brother of Yo Yo Honey Singh which seems quite clear. Money’ songs revolve around a deep house, trance and hip hop baseline with kicks from Punjabi songs and Bhangra music. He loves his music and makes it so that you love it too.

His newest album, Trance Around The World or simply TATW is a collaboration with DJ, Armin Vaan Buuren and makes it a head banging experience for all listeners. Check it out
